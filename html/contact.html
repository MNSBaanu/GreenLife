Frequently Asked Questions

We're open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and on Sundays from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

You can book an appointment by calling us, using our online booking system, or by visiting our center in person.

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments. Health insurance may cover some of our services.

While we're not an emergency facility, we do accommodate urgent cases when possible. Please call ahead.

Please bring any medical records, a list of medications, and wear comfortable clothing suitable for movement.
