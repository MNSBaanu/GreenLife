Today's Wellness Tip

Start your morning with 10 minutes of mindful breathing to energize your body and calm your mind. This simple practice can help reduce stress hormones by up to 30% and improve focus throughout your day. Try sitting comfortably with your eyes closed, breathing deeply through your nose for 4 counts, holding for 2 counts, and exhaling for 6 counts.
